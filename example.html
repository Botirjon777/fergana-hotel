Our Story

A Hotel That
Defies Convention

Antigravity Hotel was born from a single, audacious question: what if a hotel could make you feel weightless? Set high above the city in a building that seems to float against the skyline, we have crafted an experience where every detail conspires to lift your spirit.

Each of our 87 rooms is a sanctuary of calm — where light pours freely, textures breathe, and the boundaries between inside and outside dissolve into pure presence. We believe luxury is not about excess; it is about the absence of weight.
